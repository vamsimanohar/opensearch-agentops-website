## Welcome to OpenSearch - Observability Stack

OpenSearch Observability Stack is a comprehensive, open-source platform for full-stack observability. Monitor your services, infrastructure, and AI agents from a single pane of glass.

## What You Can Do

### Send Data
Instrument your applications and infrastructure with OpenTelemetry. Collect traces, logs, and metrics from any language, framework, or platform.

### Investigate
Query, explore, and correlate your observability data. Drill into traces, search logs, and analyze metrics to find root causes fast.

### Service Map & APM
Visualize service dependencies, track latency and error rates, and monitor application performance across your distributed systems.

### Dashboards & Visualize
Build custom dashboards with real-time panels, charts, and heatmaps. Share with your team and annotate with deployment markers.

### Alerts & Notifications
Define alert rules on metrics, logs, and traces. Get notified via Slack, PagerDuty, email, or webhooks. Anomaly detection and forecasting built in.

### SLOs & SLIs
Define service level objectives, track error budgets, and get burn-rate alerts. Meet your reliability targets with confidence.

### Logs
Ingest, search, and correlate logs with traces. Pattern detection, structured queries, and log-based alerting at scale.

### Metrics
Prometheus-compatible metrics ingestion with PromQL support. Custom dashboards, anomaly detection, and time-series forecasting.

### Traces
Distributed tracing with span-level detail. Trace context propagation, sampling strategies, and trace analytics.

### AI Observability
Trace AI agent workflows, run local evals, manage prompts, and track LLM costs. Full observability for your AI stack.
